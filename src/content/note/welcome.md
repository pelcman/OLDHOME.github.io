Hi, Hewwo. We are OLDHOME.

If you are reading this, it means our signal from the remote mountains of Japan or the snowy wilds of Canada has successfully survived the trip through several aging satellites to reach you.

We spend our days rescuing content that the modern internet has forgotten, and our nights wondering if we should have just become goat farmers instead.
We’re glad you’re here—stay a while, before the grid goes down.
